About

Project101, is the ultimate online destination where you'll embark on an unforgettable journey into the chaotic, hilarious, and incredibly relatable world of university life. Our channel is the brainchild of a close-knit group of five charismatic and comedic friends, and we're here to take you on a rollercoaster ride through the wild and wacky experiences we've gathered along our own university journey.

But our journey isn't just about sharing our own tales. We want to create a community where students from all walks of life can come together, and find a sense of belonging. University life can be challenging, but it's also a treasure trove of funny, endearing, and even enlightening moments. We invite you to join us on this adventure, laugh with us, and maybe even learn a thing or two along the way.
